Lecture clash training.
Anyone got a lecture clash? They're not pretty, but with your help, they can be manageable. Here are
some thoughts on how to deal with it:

  • Realise that it is up to you. You are responsible for making it work
  • Attend one lecture and get notes from the other (start with the web noes, but that is not
    enough)
  • Find a friend in the one you miss and get them to tell you what else was covered in class that's
    not on the web
  • Which one do you miss? - Some ideas to help you with that decision are:
    • If you miss one out of two lectures for a subject, that's 50%. If you miss one out of three
      that's 33%
    • Which one are you most on top of
    • Which subject has the most complete lecture notes?
  • Don't ask your lecturer - "did I miss anything in the lecture@. If you didn't miss anything,
    there would be no point in lectures.
